Projects

All my projects are available on my GitHub.
  • MNIST: I trained a neural net on the classic MNIST Digit Recognition Problemn. I also used GradCAM to visualize what features are most important for the networks decision.
  • Brain Tumor Classifier: I built a Brain Tumor Classifier using Deep Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify brain tumors on MRI Scans. Keras Xception was used as a base model which was pretrained on the ImageNet Dataset. The base model was then finetuned for brain MRI scans via transfer learning. In the end, I applied GradCAM to visualize the tumor on the scans.
    • Resources:
      • Tensorflow
      • Tensorflow Keras Xception
      • Xception paper
      • ImageNet
      • GradCAM
  • NanoGPT: I rebuilt Andrej Karpathy's NanoGPT to understand more about Large Language Models (LLMs) and the idea of transformers. It was trained on the tiny shakesspear dataset and was able to produce resonable shakesspear language.
  • Back Propagation from scratch: I implemented the Backpropagation Algorithm from scratch to understand how neural networks actually learn. This was doing the Andrej Karpathy lecture.
  • Handson-ml: I have some notebooks on the Handson ML book.
  • Lunar Lander: I build an Reinforment Learning (RL) Agent who can land the Lunar Lander. I used the Gymnasium api and Stable Baselines3 for implementation of the DQN-Algorithm.
  • Ninja Game: I built a Ninja 2D Platformer game.
