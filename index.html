Projects

All my projects are available on my GitHub.
  • MNIST: I trained a neural network on the classic MNIST Digit Recognition Problem. I also used GradCAM to visualize what features were most important for the models decision.
  • Brain Tumor Classifier: I built a Brain Tumor Classifier using Deep Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify brain tumors on MRI Scans. Keras Xception was used as a base model with pretrained ImageNet weights. The base model was then finetuned for and applied on brain MRI scans via transfer learning. In the end, GradCAM was applied to visualize the models decision and therefore the tumor on the MRI scans.
    • Resources: Tensorflow, Keras Xception, Xception paper, ImageNet, GradCAM
  • NanoGPT: I rebuilt Andrej Karpathy's NanoGPT to understand more about Large Language Models (LLMs) and the idea of transformers. It was trained on the Tiny Shakesspear dataset and was able to produce reasonable shakesspear language.
  • Back Prop from scratch: I implemented the Backpropagation Algorithm from scratch to understand how neural networks actually learn.
  • Handson-ml: I have some notebooks on the Handson ML book.
  • Reinforment Learning: I build an Reinforment Learning (RL) Agent who can land the Lunar Lander. I used the Gymnasium API and Stable Baselines3 for implementation of the DQN-Algorithm.
  • 2D Platformer Game: I built a 2D Platformer game using Pygame.
  • Strategy Game: I built a stratedy game with a team of 4 people using Maven.
  • Game Theory: I did a project on the Prisoner's Dilemma to understand which strategies actually work best.
  • XAI Research Paper: I wrote a research paper about the comparision of White and Black-Box methodes, two essential Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) approaches.
  • Apple Vision Pro Dev: During my time in San Francisco, I worked on the Apple Vision Pro.
